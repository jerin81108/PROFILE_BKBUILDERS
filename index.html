Our Specialized Scope

Surveying & Engineering Services

We offer a complete suite of licensed building surveyance, structural blueprints, legal planning permits, and cost estimations.

Licensed Building Surveyor

Official site surveys, regulatory land mapping, and legal compliance checks. We inspect and verify sites according to municipal and safety codes.

I.F. Plan & Model Plan

Drafting custom Internal Functional (I.F.) plans and 3D architectural model plans that optimize space while aligning with government regulations.

Evacuation Boards

Specialized functional planning for building safety. We design detailed fire safety escape routes and evacuation plans for commercial and residential buildings.

Precision Estimates

Comprehensive quantity surveying and cost estimation plans to give you an exact material budget and prevent hidden cost overruns during execution.

Certified Valuation

Registered structural and market valuations for buildings, helping you secure banking loans, process land registrations, and legal clearances.

All Planning Works

From foundation blueprints to multi-story structural layouts, we execute end-to-end structural designing, drawing submissions, and engineering supervisions.
